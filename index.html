Workflow

Skraak Forest Project

The goal is to build up a dataset that can be used to model all bird calls and potentially forest health.

Recording 1 minute in every 40, all day and night, every day.

  • 5 moth recorders on Pomona Island recording at 16000hz

  • 5 moth recorders on Rona Island recording at 16000hz

  • 1 moth in the Bowen Valley recording at 16000hz (2 more on the way). Thanks to Stanley Mulany and the Alpine Club.

  • 9 moths recording in South Westland

  • 7 moths recording in the Hollyford/Kaipo/Pembroke (5 more on the way).
