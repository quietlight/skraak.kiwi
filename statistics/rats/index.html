Ratiness

Ratiness is the proportion of trail cameras that made a photograph of at least 1 rat in a day.

There are usually around 12 to 14 cameras operating at any time.

Sometimes a camera will detect many rats in a day, it is not possible to know how many individual rats were detected in a day.
