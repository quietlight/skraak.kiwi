2023-11-02

I was on Pomona betweeen then 1st and 3rd of November to:

  • service 10 permanent kiwi micro moths.

  • place 3 new moths in trap boxes.

The weather was calm and beautiful.

  • Rona 1/30 Moths: 287, 343, 620, 635, 778.1 (not checked)
