2024-07-10

I rafted to Pomona on the 22nd June to:

  • service 10 permanent kiwi micro moths.

The weather was calm and beautiful on the way in, and I got a ride out thanks to the Pomona Charitable Trust and Paul and Lynley

Active Moth Locations

  • Micro Moths: A05, C05, D03, D09, F10, G05, H04, M04, N14, N20

  • Pomona 24/7 - 1/30 Moths: C03, G05, H01, J11, P09

  • Rona 24/7 - 1/30 Moths: 287, 343, 620, 635, 778.1

I was on Pomona 3 nights, mostly calm and pleasantly cold.

The batteries in N20 ran out very quickly, this may be because I carried them in pink anti static bags which I now know, conduct electricity.

There were 2346 detections, 229 or 9.7% were false positives.
