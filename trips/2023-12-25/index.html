2023-12-31

I was on Pomona over Christmas:

  • service 10 permanent kiwi micro moths.

  • collect 4 moths recording at 250_000Hz with flat batteries.

The weather was calm and beautiful.

The moth at N20 did not record anything due to a fault. It wrote its config file which means it was working when I left it, but it died subsequently.

Active Moth Locations

  • Micro Moths: C05, D03, D09, F10, G05, H04, M04, N14, N20, NB14

  • Pomona 1/30 Moths: C03, G05, H01, J11, P09 (not checked)

  • Rona 1/30 Moths: 287, 343, 620, 635, 778.1 (not checked)

The pairs at C05, D03, F10 were specially busy. I think this is because they may have a chick.
